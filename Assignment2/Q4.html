Exploring Paris

Paris, the city of love, offers an unforgettable experience with its charming streets, iconic landmarks, and delectable cuisine.

Travel Tips:

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower early to avoid long queues.
  • Try authentic croissants at local bakeries.
  • Explore the art galleries in Montmartre.
