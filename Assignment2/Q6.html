How to Make Delicious Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Toss in the cooked spaghetti and mix well.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and garnish with basil leaves.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Nutritional Information

Calories: 350 per serving

Protein: 8g

Carbohydrates: 45g

Fats: 12g

Fun Fact: Adding fresh basil not only enhances the flavor but also provides antioxidants!
